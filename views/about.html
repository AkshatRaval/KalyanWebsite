About Our Scholarship Exam

Welcome to Kalyan Educational and Charitable Trust. We are dedicated to providing quality education and support to students through our scholarship examination. Our mission is to empower students by providing financial assistance and educational resources to help them achieve their educational goals.

How the Exam Will Be Conducted

Our scholarship exam is conducted in three stages to ensure a comprehensive evaluation of the candidates' abilities and knowledge.

  1. Mains: Online test of fundamentals. Top 10,000 advance.
  2. Advanced: Challenging online test. Top 100 advance.
  3. Live Quiz: Final stage at venue. Top 100 compete live.

Preparation Tips

  • Review the syllabus thoroughly and focus on key topics.
  • Practice sample tests and mock exams.
  • Stay updated with current affairs and general knowledge.
  • Manage time effectively during preparation and the exam.

Eligibility & How to Apply

  • Currently enrolled in a recognized educational institution.
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 or equivalent.
  • Demonstrate financial need and commitment to academic excellence.
  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit academic transcripts and proof of enrollment.
  3. Provide a personal statement and recommendation letters.

Important Dates & Benefits

  • Application Deadline: June 30, 2025
  • Exam Date: July 15, 2025
  • Results Announcement: August 1, 2025
  • Financial assistance for tuition and expenses.
  • Access to resources, workshops, and networking.
  • Recognition and awards for achievements.
